About Us

The White Coat Project is a student-run medical outreach organization based in New York City. We connect high school and college students passionate about healthcare to real-world experiences through internships, volunteer opportunities, and community events.

From hospital blood drives and clinic shadowing to educational content on health topics, our mission is to empower future healthcare professionals with the tools, knowledge, and connections they need to grow.
